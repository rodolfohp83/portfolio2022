PROCESS

PLANNING
We start with a planning and survey of requirements that the client needs for their website.

We create a Wireframe to give us an idea of the structure with which we would be working.
DESIGN
We continue with the look and feel that the website will have. A prototype of the site is developed so that the client can see how the final product will be.
DEVELOPING
I build the site, with the relevant technologies, this process also serves to improve the design and the user experience.

Tests are made and observations are corrected, this on a test environment where the client can see the final product.
